About MOX VOX

Rooted in Jodhpur. Evolving with the city.

Where It Began

MOX VOX began in 2011–2012 on Sardarpura 9th D Road, Jodhpur. Founded by Rahul Katariya, it started as a modest restaurant-cum-discotheque with a basic DJ setup, professional lighting, and sound.

What began with space for around 120 diners quickly grew into a destination known for private dining and early club culture in the city.

MOX VOX Today

Today, MOX VOX operates from its rooftop location at 5th Floor, Amrit Kalash, Jodhpur—a premium restaurant and club offering fine dining with a vegetarian focus, signature cocktails, live music, and the city's most prominent club scene.

The venue has become the preferred choice for birthdays, kitty parties, corporate events, and professional nightlife.

The Experience

Under the open sky, guests experience Rajasthani beats against the city skyline, combining rooftop luxury with authentic local atmosphere. MOX VOX remains a family bar that bridges tradition and contemporary nightlife.
